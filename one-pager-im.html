The Opportunity
A $10B+ Market With a Verification Crisis
The Voluntary Carbon Market is projected to exceed $10B by 2030, with demand expected to outstrip supply (23% vs. 31% CAGR). Yet 44% of buyers now require higher verification standards, and current platforms fail to deliver. African credits alone are forecast to grow 4× by 2030, yet remain chronically underserved.
The Solution
Blockchain-Native Carbon Asset Management
  • On-chain verification: proprietary R-REC tokens on Polygon Mainnet; every MWh tracked from generation to retirement
  • ERC-20 smart contracts: with upgradeable architecture, built-in compliance controls, and transparent audit trail
  • Marketplace + Asset Hub: fully operational web platform for buying, selling, retiring, and valuing carbon assets
  • Triple-certified: B Corp, Deloitte-verified RE100, and SBTi-compliant
